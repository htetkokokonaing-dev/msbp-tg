# Claim boundary

Allowed current wording:

> MSBP-Tg is a candidate physical boundary principle for polymer Tg placement. It has support across three post-lock source families after overlap screening.

Do not write:

- natural law established,
- all glass-transition theory replaced,
- universal predictive proof,
- final mechanistic proof,
- journal-accepted result.

Use stronger wording only after independent review, manual data audit, and journal-level reproducibility checks.
