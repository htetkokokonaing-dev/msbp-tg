# Claim boundary - journal submission version

Allowed current wording:

> MSBP-Tg is a public-safe cheminformatics validation study of a mobility-suppression coordinate for polymer Tg boundary displacement. Across the reported source-family checks, the sign-reversed normalized rotatable-bond density coordinate is directionally associated with upward Tg displacement after chemistry-size residualization.

Allowed shorter wording:

> MSBP-Tg provides source-family-supported evidence for an interpretable within-fiber mobility-suppression coordinate.

Do not write:

- natural law established;
- universal Tg law proved;
- causal mechanism proved;
- all glass-transition theory replaced;
- experimental Tg measurement replaced;
- guaranteed polymer-design engine;
- new molecular descriptor invented;
- journal-accepted result before acceptance.

Required clarifications:

- MSBP density is exactly the sign reversal of normalized rotatable-bond density.
- The contribution is the coordinate framing, residualization, source-family validation, controls, and public-safe reproducibility workflow.
- Raw third-party records are not redistributed unless redistribution permission is explicit.
- Prospective experimental validation is still required for newly proposed polymer candidates.

Use stronger wording only after independent review, manual data audit, and journal-level reproducibility checks.
