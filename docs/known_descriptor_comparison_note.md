# Known descriptor comparison note 

MSBP density is the suppression-oriented sign reversal of rotatable-bond density. Therefore MSBP density and rot/heavy have equal absolute rank correlations with opposite signs by definition.

The meaningful descriptor comparisons are against alternatives such as ring density, aromatic-ring density, heteroatom density, and heavy-atom count. Ring density captures one route to mobility suppression: aromatic or cyclic rigidity. MSBP density can also respond to flexible aliphatic or side-chain mobility, so it can separate broader chemistry sources more strongly while being statistically comparable to ring density in smaller or ring-biased sources.
